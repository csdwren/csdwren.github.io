About

I join College of Computing and Intelligence at Tianjin University as an Assistant Professor. I received the B.S. degree and the M.Sc. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2011 and 2013. I received two Ph.D. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University supervised by Prof. David Zhang and Prof. Wangmeng Zuo in 2017 and 2018. My research interests include low level computer vision, deep learning and optimization methods.
